## 🎨 DESIGN SYSTEM

### Color Palette

```
Primary Background: #1a1a1a (dark gray)
Secondary Background: #2a2a2a (medium gray)
Accent Green: #00ff88 (use sparingly for CTAs and highlights)
Text White: #ffffff
Text Gray: #b0b0b0
Warning/Energy: #ffd700 (gold, for energy icons)
Data Viz: Blue #4a9eff, Purple #b366ff, Orange #ff8c42
```

### Typography

```
Slide Titles: Montserrat Bold, 44pt, White
Subtitles: Montserrat Regular, 24pt, Accent Green
Body Text: Inter Regular, 18pt, Text White
Captions: Inter Regular, 14pt, Text Gray
Numbers/Stats: Montserrat Bold, 60pt, Accent Green
```

### Icons Style

- Line icons (not filled)
- Consistent stroke weight
- Accent green color
- Sources: Lucide, Heroicons, or Feather icons

## 🎬 ANIMATION GUIDELINES

### For Digital Presentations:

**Slide Transitions:**

- Use "Push" or "Fade" (300ms)
- Avoid flashy transitions

**Content Animation:**

- Fade in stats (500ms delay between items)
- Build comparison tables row by row
- Charts: Animate from left to right
- Bullet points: Appear with fade (200ms between items)

**Don't Overdo It:**

- Max 2-3 animations per slide
- Keep it professional, not distracting

## 🎯 PRESENTATION TIPS

### Timing (for 20-minute pitch):

- Slides 1-3: 3 minutes (Problem/Opportunity)
- Slide 4: 2 minutes (Solution + demo)
- Slide 5: 2 minutes (Why We Win)
- Slides 6-7: 3 minutes (Business Model + Traction)
- Slide 8: 1 minute (Competition)
- Slides 9-10: 3 minutes (GTM + Financials)
- Slide 11: 1 minute (Team)
- Slide 12: 2 minutes (The Ask)
- Q&A: 3 minutes

### Key Delivery Notes:

- **Slide 2 (Problem):** Make it visceral - "Imagine waiting 3 months for a quote"
- **Slide 4 (Solution):** Demo if possible, or show actual quote
- **Slide 5 (Why We Win):** This is your differentiator - slow down here
- **Slide 7 (Traction):** Customer stories > numbers
- **Slide 12 (The Ask):** Be direct, confident, specific
