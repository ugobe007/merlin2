# Group 4 Filing Prep - Expansion Products and Monetization Modules

## Filing Objective

Protect the higher-value extension modules that expand Merlin beyond quoting into simulation, probabilistic finance, document ingestion, ancillary revenue modeling, vendor workflows, autonomous reliability management, subscription-gated intelligence, and zero-code vertical deployment.

## Included Disclosure Items

- Patent 5 - 8760-hour BESS dispatch simulator
- Patent 6 - Probabilistic financial analysis
- Patent 19 - Hybrid AI plus regex spec extraction
- Patent 20 - ISO/RTO ancillary revenue projection
- Patent 21 - Specialty-gated vendor portal with ML feedback loop
- Patent 22 - Usage-gated market intelligence dashboard
- Patent 24 - Self-healing wizard AI agent
- Patent 29 - Vertical SMB energy platform

## Core Story for Counsel

This group is broader than the others, so it should not be drafted as one giant omnibus application unless budget is the overriding constraint. The better framing is a product-expansion family made up of modules that all take Merlin's core quote engine and extend it into adjacent commercial functions:

- deeper technical diligence
- better financial and revenue forecasting
- supplier-side participation
- recurring SaaS monetization
- platform resilience
- white-label vertical deployment

## Best Independent Claim Themes

For a broader filing:

A computer-implemented energy-platform expansion system comprising one or more modules selected from:

- a document ingestion and structured extraction module
- a dispatch and probabilistic financial simulation module
- an ancillary services revenue projection module
- a vendor registration and RFQ routing module with approved-data feedback to quote or ML systems
- a usage-gated market intelligence presentation module

## Prior Art Positioning

Distinguish from:

- standalone battery simulators that do not connect to quoting workflows
- generic Monte Carlo tools that are not quote-integrated or do not model discrete tax-credit risk in a commercial quoting context
- generic document-AI systems that do not use hybrid AI-plus-regex extraction tailored to BESS spec fields
- generic vendor portals that do not propagate approved data into quote and ML systems
- ordinary paywalls that gate content but do not gate intelligence workflows and report execution
- generic self-healing tools focused on infrastructure rather than browser-side wizard-state remediation
- generic white-label SaaS tools that do not include per-vertical energy power profiles and runtime calculation constants

## Trade Secret Carveouts

Preserve as confidential:

- exact degradation assumptions
- exact uncertainty ranges
- exact vendor approval rules
- exact report-credit quantities by subscription tier
- exact gate-relaxation thresholds and alert windows
- exact per-vertical sizing constants and payback assumptions
