How Merlin Works

Choose your path Instant

Start with TrueQuote (for owners) or ProQuote (for vendors). Each path unlocks industry-specific logic tailored to your role and project type.

Merlin analyzes your facility AI Powered

Input location, size, and energy patterns. MerlinAI instantly pulls utility rates, solar production data, equipment costs, and available incentives for your specific location.

Build your system

Select from solar, battery storage, backup power, EV charging, or any combination. Merlin auto-sizes equipment and optimizes configurations based on your facility's unique profile.

See the full picture Instant ROI

Get comprehensive financial analysis: total cost, net investment after incentives, annual savings, payback period, 10-year ROI, carbon offset, and performance metrics.

Take action

Export to PDF, save as a quote profile, share with stakeholders, or use as your RFP foundation. Move from analysis to action with confidence.

⚡ The Magic: MerlinAI Engine

Every calculation is powered by real-time data: NREL cost benchmarks, location-specific solar production, utility rate structures, ITC/MACRS tax benefits, and degradation curves. Investment-grade accuracy in minutes.
