## Pricing Guidelines

### Battery Pricing
- **Current Range:** $140-180/kWh for LFP
- **Include:** Module cost, BMS, thermal management
- **Exclude:** Installation, shipping (quoted separately)

### Inverter Pricing
- **Current Range:** $150-220/kW
- **Include:** Hardware, control systems, grid integration
- **Exclude:** Installation, commissioning

### BOS Percentage
- **Typical:** 15-25% of equipment cost
- **Includes:** Switchgear, transformers, cables, safety

### EPC/Installation
- **Quote by:** Project complexity
- **Options:** Ground-mount vs. rooftop vs. indoor
- **Include:** Labor, permits, commissioning

## FAQ

**Q: How long does registration approval take?**  
A: Typically 24-48 hours. We review company credentials and product alignment.

**Q: Is there a fee to join the vendor network?**  
A: No. Merlin's vendor portal is free for qualified manufacturers and contractors.

**Q: How often should I update pricing?**  
A: We recommend quarterly reviews, or whenever market conditions change significantly.

**Q: Can I submit pricing for multiple product categories?**  
A: Yes! Submit separately for each product line you offer.

**Q: What happens if my pricing isn't competitive?**  
A: Your submission is still approved, but may be selected less frequently. We provide market benchmark data to help you stay competitive.

**Q: How do I get notified of new RFQs?**  
A: Email notifications are sent automatically for projects matching your specialty and capacity range.

**Q: Can I withdraw a quote after submission?**  
A: Yes, contact the vendor support team immediately. Note that quotes already sent to customers cannot be fully retracted.

**Q: What if I can't meet the lead time on an RFQ?**  
A: Submit your actual lead time. Some customers prioritize speed, others prioritize cost or quality.
