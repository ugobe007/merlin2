## The Problem

4,500 site views and zero signups is not a UX problem. It is not a wizard-vs-workspace problem. It is a category mismatch.

Commercial solar and storage projects are $500K–$5M decisions made by CFOs and facility VPs through referrals, brokers, RFPs, EPC relationships, financing partners, and board-level pressure. They are not bought self-serve from a website by a stranger at 11pm.

Even a perfect AI workspace will not change that trust threshold.

The workspace pivot in the product direction is sound. The GTM under it is not.

## The Pivot

Merlin moves from self-serve quote tool to founder-led concierge AI consulting.

The product direction stays:

- Workspace
- Agents
- TrueQuote
- MagicFit
- Merlin Brief

The go-to-market changes.

### New Positioning

> Merlin is the AI energy consultant for energy-intensive multi-site commercial operators.

Merlin does the diagnostic work for free, delivers a CFO-grade Merlin Brief in 14 days, then converts qualified opportunities into paid project management, success-fee, or channel engagements.

## The Offer: Free Merlin Energy Intelligence Audit

### Offer

> Free 2-week Merlin Energy Intelligence Audit

Terms:

- Zero cost
- Zero obligation
- Signed engagement letter on day 0
- Paid follow-on conversation on day 14

### Inputs

- 12 months of utility bills
- Site addresses
- Business type
- Business goal
- Optional interval data
- Optional site photos
- Optional tariff or utility account details

### Output: Merlin Brief

The audit produces a CFO-grade Merlin Brief with:

- Sized system recommendation
- Estimated savings
- ITC, domestic content, and energy community capture screen
- Payback range
- EBITDA-per-site reframe
- Portfolio-level opportunity view where applicable
- Demand-charge or tariff exposure where applicable
- Missing-data and blocker list
- Recommended next steps
