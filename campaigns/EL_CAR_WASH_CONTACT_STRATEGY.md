### Initial Discovery Call (15-30 minutes)

**Objective:** Qualify interest, understand priorities, secure site walk

**Agenda:**
1. **Intro (2 min):** Brief background on Merlin + alpha partnership program
2. **Their Situation (5 min):** Ask about energy costs, sustainability goals, pain points
3. **Our Solution (8 min):** Walk through quote highlights, key metrics
4. **Q&A (10 min):** Answer questions, address concerns
5. **Next Steps (5 min):** Propose site walk with EPC partners

**Key Questions to Ask:**
1. "What are your biggest energy cost pain points right now?"
2. "Have you explored solar or battery storage before? What stopped you?"
3. "Is sustainability / ESG a priority for El Car Wash or your PE partners?"
4. "What's your typical decision process for a $2M+ capital project?"
5. "Who else needs to be involved in this evaluation? (CFO, PE partner, etc.)"
6. "What would make this a no-brainer for you?"
7. "Are you open to being an alpha partner / case study for PR value?"

- "We're selling the company in next 12 months" (short timeline)
- "We don't have budget authority" (need to go higher)
- "Solar doesn't work for car washes" (address misconception immediately)

**Green Lights (Qualifiers):**
- "We're frustrated with high demand charges" ✅
- "We want to differentiate our brand with sustainability" ✅

- "Our PE partners are focused on operational efficiency" ✅

### Objection #3: "What if equipment fails?"

**Response:**
```
Great question. Here's how we de-risk equipment failure:

**Warranties:**
• Solar panels: 25-year performance warranty (Tier 1 manufacturers only)
• Batteries: 10-year warranty, 4,000 cycles @ 80% depth of discharge
• Inverters: 10-year standard, 25-year available
• Workmanship: 10-year installation warranty from EPC

**Insurance:**
• Builder's risk during construction
• Property insurance post-installation (hurricane, fire, theft)
• Business interruption coverage (if system fails, insurance pays lost savings)

**Performance Guarantees:**
• EPC guarantees system output within 95% of modeled savings
• If underperforms, they refund the difference or add capacity

**Redundancy:**
• Grid-tied system = if battery fails, you still have grid power
• <16ms transfer time = seamless (equipment doesn't even notice)

Bottom line: You're protected on all sides. This isn't bleeding-edge tech — 
it's proven, bankable, and fully warranted.
```

**Response:**
```
Actually, Florida wind-load requirements make this equipment MORE resilient 
than standard installations:

**Wind Rating:**
• 150+ mph wind-rated (exceeds Cat 4 hurricane requirements)
• Florida Building Code compliant (strictest in US)

**Track Record:**
• Solar installations survived Hurricane Ian (2022), Hurricane Irma (2017)

• Insurance companies actually LOWER premiums for facilities with backup power

**Bonus:**
The battery gives you instant backup power during grid outages. When hurricanes 
knock out power for 3-5 days, your competitors are closed — you're operational. 
That's a competitive advantage.

**Real Example:**
After Hurricane Ian, a Florida Wawa with solar+battery stayed open while 
competitors were dark for a week. They made 5X normal revenue serving the

```

### Objection #5: "We can't finance $2M right now"

**Response:**
```
No problem — we have multiple financing options that require zero upfront capital:

**Option 1: PPA (Power Purchase Agreement)**
• $0 down
• You buy power from us at a discount to your current rate (e.g., $0.09/kWh vs $0.12/kWh)
• We own/maintain equipment for 20 years
• Savings start Day 1, no capex required

**Option 2: Debt Financing (70-80% LTV)**
• Banks love solar+battery (tangible asset, predictable cash flow)
• 6-8% interest rates (equipment is collateral)
• Levered IRR jumps to 28%+
• Debt service covered by savings (cash-flow positive from Day 1)

**Option 3: Tax Equity Partnership**
• We bring in a tax equity investor to monetize the 54% ITC
• You retain majority ownership + operational control
• Reduces effective payback to 2-3 years on your equity

**Option 4: C-PACE (Commercial Property Assessed Clean Energy)**
• 20-year financing tied to property (not balance sheet)
• Non-recourse (doesn't impact borrowing capacity)
• Paid via property tax assessment (transferable to buyer if you sell)

We can model all 4 scenarios and show you which optimizes your returns.
```
