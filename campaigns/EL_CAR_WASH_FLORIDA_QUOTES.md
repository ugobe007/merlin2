## APPENDIX: ASSUMPTIONS & METHODOLOGY

### Power Calculations
- **Car wash load profile:** Industry standard kW/bay ratios from ASHRAE, CBECS
- **Peak demand:** 35-45 kW per bay (tunnel wash with full service)
- **Duty cycle:** 55% average (10am-6pm peak hours)
- **Seasonal variation:** ±15% (summer AC loads vs. winter)

### Solar Sizing
- **Coverage ratio:** 75% of peak load (conservative for carport constraints)
- **Production:** 1,700 kWh/kW/year (Florida average, south-facing tilt-optimized)
- **Degradation:** 0.5% per year (Tier 1 modules)
- **Shading losses:** 3% (carport structure, trees)

### Battery Sizing
- **Duration:** 2.5-3 hours (peak shaving focus)
- **Power:** 60% of peak load (typical demand charge optimization)
- **Round-trip efficiency:** 92% (LFP chemistry)
- **Degradation:** 2% per year, 80% capacity @ 10 years
- **Warranty:** 10 years, 4,000 cycles @ 80% DoD
