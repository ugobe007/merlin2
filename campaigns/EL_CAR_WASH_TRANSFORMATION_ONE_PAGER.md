## APPENDIX: TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

### Solar Carport Systems
- **Modules:** Tier 1 bifacial (Longi, JA Solar, Trina)
- **Power:** 450-550W per panel
- **Mounting:** Aluminum or galvanized steel structure
- **Wind Rating:** 150+ mph (ASCE 7-16, Florida Building Code)
- **Clearance:** 8-10 ft height (accommodate trucks, RVs)
- **Warranty:** 25 years performance, 12 years product

### Battery Energy Storage Systems
- **Chemistry:** LFP (lithium iron phosphate) - safest, longest life
- **Capacity:** 2.5-4 hour duration (site-specific)
- **Efficiency:** 92-94% round-trip
- **Warranty:** 10 years, 4,000 cycles @ 80% DoD, 80% capacity retention
- **Safety:** UL 9540A tested, NFPA 855 compliant
- **Lifespan:** 15-20 years typical

### Backup Generators
- **Fuel:** Natural gas (preferred) or dual-fuel (NG + diesel)
- **Size:** 125-200kW (site-specific)
- **Transfer Time:** <200ms with BESS, <10 seconds without
- **Manufacturer:** Cummins, Caterpillar, Kohler
- **Warranty:** 5 years / 2,000 hours

### EV Charging Infrastructure
- **Level 2:** 7.2kW (ChargePoint, Electrify America)
- **DCFC:** 150-350kW (Tritium, ABB, BTC Power)
- **Network:** ChargePoint or Electrify America (revenue sharing)
- **Payment:** Credit card, mobile app, RFID
- **Warranty:** 3 years parts + labor
